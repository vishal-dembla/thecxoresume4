Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our bespoke executive resume writing process.

Understanding the Service

What is The CXO Resume and how is it different from traditional resume services?

Most services give you formatting. We give you framing. The CXO Resume is founder-led, bespoke, and narrative-driven. We cut through titles and dates to surface what truly matters: your leadership arc. The difference is simple. Others document the past. We position you for the future.

Why focus on a 'leadership narrative' instead of a chronological list?

Because lists are history. Narratives are strategy. A leadership narrative makes sense of inflection points, shows judgment in action, and connects what you have done to where you are going. It transforms a record into a roadmap.

What does 'bespoke' and 'founder-led' mean here?

Bespoke means no templates. Every word is earned, every structure tailored. Founder-led means your story is not outsourced. It is shaped directly by someone who has spent decades in executive search and boardrooms, with the precision of lived context.

What is your approach to writing executive resumes?

It is editorial rigor applied to careers. Intelligent restraint. Sharp sentences that carry weight. No clutter, no filler, no clichés. The result is a document that reads less like a ledger and more like a statement of leadership.

Who Benefits

Who are your ideal clients?

CXOs in transition, board aspirants, founders post-exit, senior military leaders stepping into corporate life, and executives taking global roles. In short, leaders at turning points where clarity of story shapes access to opportunity.

Can you help executives with non-linear careers?

Yes. In fact, we thrive on complexity. Non-linear paths—entrepreneurship, cross-border pivots, portfolio careers—become liabilities when reduced to lists. Through narrative, they become proof of agility, breadth, and vision.

Is this service for early-career professionals?

No. Early careers are about entry points. Our work begins where entry ends: at the level where positioning matters more than chronology, and narrative matters more than formatting.

The Process

What does the discovery call involve?

It is not a sales pitch. It is a conversation about context: what you seek, what drives the transition, what the document must achieve. It frames the work ahead.

What happens in the deep-dive interview?

It is not interrogation. It is excavation. We uncover the choices, the pivots, the inflection points. The stories you overlook because they feel obvious often prove decisive. That is where we dig.

How long does the process take?

Two to three weeks. Enough time to interview, write, and refine. Short enough to serve urgent needs, long enough to honor editorial craft.

How do feedback and iterations work?

You receive a draft within ten days. We refine together, cutting, sharpening, and aligning. The final version is not ours. It is yours—shaped with you, not for you.

Deliverables & Outcomes

What do I receive?

A bespoke executive resume, architected from scratch. Alongside it, a positioning narrative: the through-line of your leadership, distilled into language that commands attention.

What optional add-ons exist?

LinkedIn profiles rewritten with narrative integrity. Board bios designed for investors and nominations. Advisory on how to extend the narrative into media, decks, and speeches.

How can the positioning narrative be used beyond the resume?

It becomes a master key. One story, told consistently, opens many doors—LinkedIn, board bios, interviews, media introductions. Multiplying your voice without fragmenting it.

What outcomes can I expect?

We do not promise jobs. What we deliver is sharper positioning with search firms, stronger resonance with boards, and greater confidence in interviews. The outcome is clarity. Opportunity follows.

Why call this an investment, not an expense?

Because an executive role is measured in millions—of dollars, of influence, of outcomes. Clarity in how you present yourself compounds returns for years. That is investment.

Addressing Common Concerns

I already have a resume. Why do I need another?

Most executive resumes read like obituaries: long, heavy, past tense. What you need is a positioning document—short, sharp, alive with purpose. That is the rewrite we deliver.

How do you uncover themes I may have missed?

Because you lived them, they feel ordinary. To us, they are extraordinary. Through structured questioning, we surface decisions and pivots that reveal your leadership DNA.

Is this ghostwriting, or is my input crucial?

It is collaboration. We bring structure, language, perspective. You bring substance, voice, authenticity. The alchemy lies in partnership, not substitution.

How do you ensure relevance across industries and roles?

Context matters. We adapt narrative to align with sector, geography, and audience. Having worked with search firms and boards globally, we know the languages of finance, technology, social impact, and beyond.
