Confidentiality Agreement

Our commitment to protecting your career information and professional reputation.

This Confidentiality Agreement governs the protection of sensitive information shared between you (the "Client") and The CXO Resume (the "Service Provider") during our professional engagement.

Executive-Level Discretion

We understand that C-suite career transitions require the highest levels of confidentiality. This agreement ensures your information remains secure throughout our engagement.

Scope of Confidential Information

Career Information: All details about your professional history, current employment status, career transitions, compensation, and future aspirations.

Company Information: Any proprietary or sensitive information about current or former employers, including strategic initiatives, financial performance, or internal matters.

Personal Information: Contact details, family considerations affecting career decisions, relocation preferences, and other personal circumstances.

Strategic Information: Your job search strategy, target companies, networking contacts, interview processes, and negotiation details.

Our Confidentiality Commitments

Non-Disclosure: We will never disclose your identity, career information, or any details of our engagement to third parties without your explicit written consent.

Secure Storage: All client information is stored using encrypted, password-protected systems with restricted access.

Limited Access: Only authorized team members directly involved in your project have access to your information.

Professional Discretion: We maintain complete discretion in all communications and will never reference your engagement in marketing materials without permission.

No Case Study Use: Your specific information will not be used in case studies, testimonials, or examples without your explicit written consent.

Communication Security

Secure Channels: All communications are conducted through encrypted email systems and secure file-sharing platforms.

Meeting Confidentiality: Discovery calls and meetings are conducted in private settings with no recordings unless specifically requested and approved by you.

Reference Checks: Any contact with references or professional contacts will only occur with your explicit prior approval and specific instructions.

Information Retention and Disposal

Retention Period: Client information is retained for up to three years after project completion to provide ongoing support and potential updates.

Secure Deletion: After the retention period, all personal and confidential information is securely deleted from our systems.

Early Deletion: You may request immediate deletion of your information at any time, which we will complete within 30 days.

Team Member Obligations

All team members involved in client services are bound by individual confidentiality agreements and professional standards that extend beyond the duration of their employment with The CXO Resume.

Duration and Contact

This confidentiality agreement remains in effect indefinitely and survives the completion of our professional engagement. For questions about this agreement or to discuss specific confidentiality concerns, contact us at confidential@thecxoresume.com

Questions About Confidentiality?

We're committed to maintaining the highest standards of discretion for all executive clients. Contact us to discuss any specific confidentiality requirements.
